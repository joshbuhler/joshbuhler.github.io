About me

Hey. I’m Josh.

I’m just an average guy doing my thing out here in Herriman, Utah. I’ve been married to a beautiful girl for a while now, and together we’ve got three pretty awesome kids. There’s also a cat that for the most part, hates the world, but likes us for some reason.

My day job is at Control4, working as an iOS developer. Basically, we make some pretty killer home automation gear. Lights, audio, home theaters, 8-jet car wash style showers, you name it, we can probably already control it, and help you feel like you live in the Jetson’s house. If not, we’ll likely be able to enable control for it soon. It’s a sweet gig.

In the past, I’ve worked on games, a pile of mobile apps, and another heap of web apps. Basically, I’m a professional computer geek. I’m also a bit of a ham radio nerd, and you can find me on the air as KC6BSA.

When I’m not doing that, I’m usually out with the family, camping, playing some ice hockey, or just screwing around with one project or another. Occasionally, I even write stuff.
